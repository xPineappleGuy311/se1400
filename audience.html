General Audience of the game:

The general audience for Guitar Hero spans a wide age range, typically from teens to adults in their 30s and 40s. The game appeals to casual players who enjoy rhythm-based fun and music fans who appreciate rock and classic hits. Competitive players and long-time enthusiasts often include those who grew up with the franchise in the 2000s, while younger players discover it through nostalgia or streaming platforms. Its demographic leans slightly male, but it has a diverse player base united by a love for music and gaming.
